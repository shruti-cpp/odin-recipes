Brownie with Ice-Cream

Description

Hi, This is quite geniunely my go-to, all time favorite recipe. im geniunely addicted it's literally the easiest recipeyou'll ever find also it jus takes 5 min to make.

Ingredients list

  • 2 packet choco biscuit
  • 2 big tpsp sugar
  • vanilla escense
  • milk

STEPS

  1. crumble the biscuits into a bowl
  2. add all the Ingredients and grind it in a grinder
  3. put this batter in a microwable bowl
  4. bake it for 10 mins
  5. Youre done with the brownie part
  6. Add one scoope of icecream on top of it and enjoy the dessert
