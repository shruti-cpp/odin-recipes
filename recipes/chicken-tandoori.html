Chicken Tandoori

Description

Hi, this is the easiest yet tastiest chicken recipefor ya'll. ive made it so many times yet im never bored of it. my go to chicken recipe.

Ingredients list

  • Chicken
  • 200gm dahi
  • 2 tpsp chilli powder
  • 1 tpsp tumeric powder
  • 1 tpsp dhaniya powder
  • oil

STEPS

  1. take a full chicken and cut into pieneces
  2. make deep cuts in chicken so that it marinates well
  3. add 200gms dahi,1tpsp tumeric powder,butter,2tpsp chilli powder and mix it all well
  4. keep the chicken to marinate for 30 mins
  5. add a bit of water uptil the level of ice cubes
  6. after 30 mins, set your airfryer to 120 degrees and preheat
  7. oil well the airfryer, set temperature to 160-200 degrees and keep the chicken peices in for 10 mins
  8. after 10 mins turn over the chicken for it well to roast on both sides
  9. And you're done with your chicken, enjoy w hot chilli sauce
