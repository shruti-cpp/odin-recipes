Iced Coffee!

Description

Hi, This is quite geniunely my go-to, all time favorite recipe. im geniunely addicted it's literally the easiest recipeyou'll ever find also it jus takes 5 min to make.

Ingredients list

  • 2 tpsp coffee powder
  • 2 tpsp sugar
  • 12 ice cubes
  • milk

STEPS

  1. take a mason jar and add 2 tpsp coffee in it
  2. add 2tpsp sugar in the jar
  3. add few drops of water and mix it well until it disoolves
  4. add 6 icecubes and mix well
  5. add a bit of water uptil the level of ice cubes
  6. fill the left over jar with milk (Note: dont fill upto the brim.)
  7. add 6 more ice-cubes and mix it well
  8. Add a cute lil straw and Voila! your iced coffee is ready to drink.
